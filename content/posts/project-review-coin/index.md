## Introduction

[Coin](https://github.com/stpotter16/coin) is a tool I built to help me manage my personal finances.

It allows users to track their income and spending by categorizing spending into two categories

**Fixed** - the monthly amount is the same every month or known ahead of time (rent, car payment, etc.)

**Flexible** - where the monthly amount varies each month (groceries, gas, etc.)


## Why build Coin

I've used zero based budgeting in in the past, but felt like it made me miss the forest through the trees.

The question I care _most_ about when managing my money is that I don't spend more than I make, not whether the popcorn I bought at the movie theater is "Entertainment" or "Eating Out"
